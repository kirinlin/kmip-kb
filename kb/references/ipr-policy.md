# IPR Policy

## Summary

KMIP 1.4 is published under the OASIS IPR Policy in its **RF on RAND**
(royalty-free on reasonable-and-non-discriminatory terms) mode — the mode the
KMIP Technical Committee selected at its founding. In practice this means
implementers can build conforming KMIP products without negotiating patent
royalties from TC members, subject to the policy's conditions. KMIP 1.4 was
the first 1.x release to carry this statement as its own numbered section
(§1.0 then; v2.1 §1.1); earlier versions carried equivalent notices on their
title pages.

## Entries

- The legal text lives in the OASIS IPR Policy itself, not in the KMIP spec;
  the section merely declares which mode applies.
- Patent disclosures and licensing offers, if any, are tracked on the KMIP
  TC's IPR page at OASIS — check it rather than the spec for current status.
